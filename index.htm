About BIT DURG

Bhilai Institute of technology Trust in 1986. BIT, set up as the first self-financed Engineering College in the erstwhile Madhya Pradesh has now shaped as one of the most sought after professional institutes in the central region. BIT is standing tall and steadfast in its commitment to provide qualitative education for the students. The courses offered by BIT are approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
